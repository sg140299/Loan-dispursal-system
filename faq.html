FAQ

What is Sign up?

User are required to complete sign up process to register on LoanMe portal. Only User who have completed "Sign up" / "Registration" process can apply for loan through LoanMe portal.


Why is my loan application rejected?

You may not be eligible as per the norms laid down by the selected banks. Please contact the selected banks for more details.


Are there any hidden charges?

There are no hidden charges. Our Group NBFC levy 2% of sanctioned amount as processing fees


How much time does it take to get credit evaluation done?

Our Group NBFC takes 1 to 3 working days.


What are the all documents I need to get credit evaluation done?

College registration letter or College admission letter
